My Hobbies

I am always looking to learn something new. I have had many hobbies over the years, but here are a few of my current hobbies that I do when I am not at work or doing homework.

Music

Probably the biggest hobby I have is music. I love music. From a very early age I learned to play the piano. I grew up with my grandpa, who played the piano by ear very well. I later took 6 years of piano lessons growing up, and was constantly playing. I inherited my grandpa's ability to play by ear. I was once grounded from playing the piano, because it was distracting me from doing homework. Sounds silly, but it was absolutely reasonable at the time. I don't play as much now, but still spend a fair amount of time playing.

I also play the guitar. Between the guitar and piano, I could burn an entire day playing around. I taught myself to play the guitar and have written a few songs.

Woodworking

I aquired my interest in woodworking during my high school years. I took woodshop classes nearly every year starting in 8th grade.

I make a lot of things, but the last few years I have been making Native American Flutes. I started making flutes about 4 year ago, and eventually found my way to making branch flutes.

The branch flutes I make are made after the Native American Flute style. Over the last few years I have grown the hobby into a small business. I don’t sell very many flutes, because of school and my day job, but I hope to one day make a significant portion of my income off of my woodworking projects. Here is a link to my website where I sell my flutes BranchFlutes.com.

Gardening

Gardening is one of my favorite hobbies. It only comes second to music, but it is a very close second. I developed a love for gardening from my dad, who always had a beautiful vegetable garden growing. I don't grow anything too fancy, I just enjoy growing good vegetables to feed my family during the summer. The picture to the left is of last years garden.
